Guv's Collection

Company Website Project

For this particular project I reached out to a small business and requested the opportunity to create a prototype website set for launch in August 2020. Since I was starting from scratch I visited the business which was a haberdashery wholesalers on the regular to gain some inspiration. I then went from paper straight to coding. I made this website responsive as to not restrict views to only desktop users. I included some low quality images of stock to create a sense of layout and to give the owner their own inspiration to decide what kind of images they would like to include before the site goes live. Also as there are so many vibrant colours and eye-catching images I also included some slow transitions as not to overwhelm the user looking at the site.
